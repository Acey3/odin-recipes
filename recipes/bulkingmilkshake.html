Banana Shake

Here is a brief description of a banana milkshake

This banana milkshake recipe is a delectable treat! It's creamy, rich, and sweet. I recommend this for a morning drink.Takes a total time of 10 minutes to prepare.

Ingredients to be used to make the Shake:

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 scoop vanilla ice cream
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon, or to taste
  • 2 tablespoons whipped cream or more to taste
  • 1 tablespoon caramel topping

HOW TO MAKE A BANANA SHAKE

Here's how to make a banana shake

  1. Combine milk, ice cream, banana, and cinnamon in a blender;
  2. Blend on low speed until the mixture reaches the consistency of a thick milkshake.
  3. Pour milkshake into a cup and top with whipped cream and drizzled caramel.
